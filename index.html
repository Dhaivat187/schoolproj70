The school campus has a wonderful basketball ground next to the building. Extra-curricular basketball classes are held here. There is also an integrated tennis and badminton court. During the recess, this is a cool place to hang out and play with your classmates.

Our Achievments


Winning the State Football Championship

In 1992 our school had won the State Football Championship of Maharashtra. We had won by 20-17.


Winning the National Elocution Contest

In 2014, 14 children from different grades had gone to Delhi to take part in the National Elocution Contest. 10 of the had won gold medals and two had won silver for their age groups. Our school was awarded the first prize.


Bagging the second prize in swimming

In 2019, Chetan Dolankar from 6th grade had represented our school in the State Swimming Championship and won the silver medal.
